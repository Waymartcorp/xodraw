# Vision

## Why XODraw exists
In youth sports, games often turn on **1–2 repeatable plays** that exploit a strength or a weakness. Coaches can see the edge, but communicating it fast—especially under time pressure—is hard.

XODraw reduces that friction by converting spoken intent into a simple diagram that players can understand quickly.

## North Star
**A coach creates a usable diagram in under 20 seconds** and can show it to players immediately.

## What “usable” means
- Clean, high-contrast, readable at arm’s length on a phone
- Minimal signifiers (players, puck/ball, arrows)
- No clutter

## How it grows later (not now)
- Role-specific spoken cues
- Practice “decks” (sequence of plays/drills)
- Strength/weakness tags + simple suggestions
- Scouting-to-counter workflows
